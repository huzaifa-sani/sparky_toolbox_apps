# Sparky - Electrical Calculator ⚡

**Electrical Calculator with 9 Calculators for Electricians & Contractors**

Stop wasting time with manual paperwork or complex formulas. Built specifically for electrical contractors and field electricians, this app delivers fast, on-the-job preliminary calculations right from your phone. 

Whether you are estimating a project, bidding a job, or verifying field measurements, these 9 powerful calculators streamline your workflow and save you time.

---

## 🚀 9 Essential Calculators in One App

*   **Ohms Law:** Instantly solve for voltage, current, and power.
*   **Motor OCPD & Wire Sizing:** Determine Overcurrent Protection Devices and wire sizes for motors.
*   **Transformer OCPD & Wire Sizing:** Get breaker and conductor sizing for transformer loads.
*   **Wire Selection & Sizing:** Select wire size and matching OCPD for any specific application.
*   **Conduit Fill:** Check total wire capacity limits for your conduit runs.
*   **Voltage Drop:** Estimate voltage loss over long distances to keep your systems efficient.
*   **Standard Dwelling Load:** Simplify residential service calculations in seconds.
*   **Transformer Sizing:** Determine the kVA rating needed for your specific load requirements.
*   **Solar Panel Calculator:** Input energy targets to find how many PV panels you will need.
